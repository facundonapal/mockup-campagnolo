subject Complete description add_circleremove_circle

Standing on the shoulders of Campagnolo’s summer/spring version of the arm warmer the ProTemp Winter Arm Warmer incorporates all of the performance tech and design and incorporates it into a warmer, thermal solution for use in more severe temperatures and conditions. By remaining faithful to the two most important redeeming qualities of the summer/spring arm warmers, low profile protection and performance minded anatomic cut, the winter version is flexible and allows for great freedom of movement. Additional treatments to the thermal fabrics used in the construction of these arm warmers render them resistant to both water and wind.

This sleek, low profile protection means less wrinkles or bunching but also contributes to the extreme packability of the garment. Warmer than the summer-weight version but more packable than other winter arm warmers available in the marketplace the ProTemp Winter Arm Warmer can always find a spot in a jersey pocket.

Packable, efficient and performance oriented thermal protection that adds a high degree of versatility to any riders outfit. Conditions can change over the course of a ride. Be prepared for whatever comes with ProTemp Winter Arm Warmers.

Fit

RACE Fit

Items with anatomic, ergonomic cut for demanding cyclists.

Season

Winter

The items in the Campagnolo winter collection ensure protection against the elements, breathability and great freedom of movement. Innovative fabrics and a specially developed design allow the cyclist to pedal in even the most extreme conditions.

See all Winter products arrow_right

Benefits

Thermal

Specifically designed for protection from cold, thermal items offer great breathability and a comfortable fit at the same time. The special fabrics can offer extremely high protection despite their very limited thickness. Heat regulation is guaranteed by the special positioning of the fibres.

See all Thermal products arrow_right

Technologies

High visibility

Safety is important and passion can’t be reined in. With Campagnolo’s high-visibility items, now you can cycle in greater safety also in the evening or in conditions of low visibility. They employ special extremely reflective sections, effectively positioned to meet both safety and design requirements.

Durable water resistant treatment

DWR treatment causes water to slip over the fabric and prevents saturation of moisture in the external fabric of clothing items. At a microscopic level, the chains of molecules in DWR treatment bind with the surface of the fabric and act as a series of needles that keep water as beads that roll over the surface.
